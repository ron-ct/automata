An elevator(lift) machine helps people navigate the ten floors at the
JKUAT towers in Nairobi city. The machine waits for students and/or
staff to press the floor they wish to go to and then assigns them entry
doors. Three doors A, B and C alight at different floors of the building as
follows:-
Door A serves ground floor upto 5th floor.
Door B serves ground floor upto 8th floor.
Door C serves ground floor upto 10th floor.


Using a programming language of your choice write a
computer program to implement the above machine.

Simulate this problem with an appropriate User Interface
